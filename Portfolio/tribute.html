Lebron James

The King of Basketball

Here's a time line of Lebron James' career:

  • 1984 - Born in Akron, Ohio
  • 2003 - Drafted First overall by the Cleveland Cavaliers in the NBA draft"
  • 2004 - Named NBA Rookie of the Year
  • 2010 - Announces decision to join Miami Heat, winning two NBA championships in four seasons
  • 2014 - Returns to Cleveland Cavaliers, leading the team to a championship in 2015
  • 2018 - Sings with the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2020 - Wins his fourth NBA championship with the Lakers
  • 2021 - Becomes first NBA player to accumulate $1 billion in earnings while still active in the sport
  • 2023 - Lebron Passes kareen Abdul-Jabbar for NBA career scoring record.
  • 2024 - Became the first player to hit 40,000 points in the NBA.
  • Present - Still active in the league playing with the Los Angeles Lakers
